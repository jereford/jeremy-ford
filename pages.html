Pages featuring Sadat X hip hop song and artwork

Artwork for "Pages," a self-produced hip hop song featuring Sadat X of Brand Nubian. I designed a handmade collage to represent the various references to paper made throughout the track. This aesthetic was also a visual representation of the art of sampling, where pieces of existing music are cut and reassembled to create an entirely new composition.

To tease the song prior to its release, I created a series of three images for Instagram. Each image utilized a cropped portion of the collage and featured an impactful and memorable lyric from the song.
