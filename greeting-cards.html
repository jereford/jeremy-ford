Greeting cards

Several years ago, I wandered into a large convenience store (the name of which shall remain anonymous, but it may have rhymed with Talljeans) in hopes of finding a greeting card for a family member that was 1) well designed, 2) well written, and 3) reasonably priced. As I read what felt like the 100th card, I realized I wasn't going to find one that met all of these criteria. I put it back (in the wrong place, out of spite) and promised myself I would never purchase another. Since that day, I've created dozens of cards for family and friends. These are some of my favorites.
