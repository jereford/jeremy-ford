Columbia Business School: Annual Dinner website

Proposed design for a responsive site promoting the school's premier fundraising event. The Annual Dinner is a private black tie occasion that brings together 800 alumni, faculty members, students and friends to recognize, celebrate and support invitiatives like student financial aid, curriculum development and faculty research. The site accompanied a suite of branded print materials including an invitation, save the date, reply card, menu, program and stationery.
